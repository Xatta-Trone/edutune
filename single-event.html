Watching Their Dust: Photographing Players in Pollination

Fun for all the family! Come along to Harcourt Arboretum for our Autumn Fair. Immerse yourself in wonderful woodland stories, enjoy traditional English folk music, taste and take away some delicious local produce. Children can have a go at carving a pencil, willow weaving or even make their own bug hotel and lots of things.

Harcourt Arboretum is made up of 130 acres containing the best collection of trees in Oxfordshire with some of the oldest redwoods in the UK.Gates open at 11am. Please note that car parking for the event is limited. We encourage our visitors to consider car-sharing or using public.

Venue :Columbus Place and Alumni Center

Location :716 Columbus Ave. Boston, MA 02120 US

Time :6:00 pm - 9:00 pm

Date:October 16,2017
